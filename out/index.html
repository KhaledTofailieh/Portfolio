My Journey

I work at the intersection of AI Engineering and Backend Architecture, creating systems that are both smart and scalable. My background started with data engineering and machine learning models, which naturally led me to building production-grade AI systems. Over the years, I have designed and deployed complete platforms from scratch—like Subul's Talent Engine—taking them from initial ideas to actual production serving thousands of active users.

For me, AI is not just about using APIs. It is about building smart, autonomous workflows and search systems that solve real-world problems. Whether creating hybrid semantic search engines with Qdrant and Reciprocal Rank Fusion (RRF), or orchestrating multi-agent systems with LangGraph, I focus on writing clean code and using Domain-Driven Design (DDD). I enjoy finding and fixing technical bottlenecks to make sure systems are reliable and fast.

Continuous learning and sharing knowledge are very important to me. When I am not working on my MSc in Artificial Intelligence or writing articles on Medium, I teach and mentor other developers in programs like the 'AI Innovator Pathway'. I love experimenting with new technologies and pushing the boundaries of what intelligent software can do.
